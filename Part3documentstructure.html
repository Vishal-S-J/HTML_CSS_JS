One fact about Eagles

Eagles are one of the largest and strongest birds in the sky.
They are nicknamed “birds of prey” due to their gracefulness, balanced aggression, and fantastic eyesight.
They are extraordinary hunters with extremely impressive wingspan.
Also, they stand as a living symbol of freedom, power, and transcendence worldwide.

Diet of Eagles

These apex predators are carnivorous, making it comfortable to eat other animals, including fish.
